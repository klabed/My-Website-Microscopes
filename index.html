About Microscopes

Modern microscopes let us see tiny structures and materials. This site provides a short overview of common microscope types and resources to learn more.

Types

  • Optical microscope — uses visible light and lenses.
  • Electron microscope — uses electron beams for higher resolution.
  • Scanning probe microscope — senses the surface with a probe.

Types of Microscopes

Light Microscopes

Traditional microscopes using visible light to examine specimens.

Electron Microscopes

Advanced microscopes using electron beams for extremely high magnification.

Scanning Probe Microscopes

Modern microscopes that create images by "feeling" the surface with a mechanical probe.
